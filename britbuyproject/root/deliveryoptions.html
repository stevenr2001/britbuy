UK Delivery Options

At Brit-Buy, all our products are shipped directly from within the UK, meaning faster delivery times compared to ordering from international marketplaces directly. Here are our current delivery options:

Standard UK Delivery

  • Cost: £3.99 (Free on orders over £50)
  • Estimated Delivery Time: 3-5 working days after dispatch.
  • Details: Orders are typically dispatched within 1-2 working days. Delivery is handled by Royal Mail or equivalent couriers.

Express UK Delivery

  • Cost: £6.99
  • Estimated Delivery Time: 1-2 working days after dispatch.
  • Details: Orders placed before 1 PM Monday-Friday are typically dispatched the same day. Delivery is handled by tracked courier services.

Important Notes

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on location and courier performance.
  • Working days exclude weekends and UK public holidays.
  • You will receive a dispatch confirmation email once your order is on its way. Express orders will include tracking information.
  • Currently, we only ship to addresses within the United Kingdom.

If you have any questions about delivery, please don't hesitate to contact us.
